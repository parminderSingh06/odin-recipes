Butter Chicken

Description:

Tender and juicy chicken thanks to a deliciously spiced yogurt marinade,
Butter Chicken is ridiculously easy to make. Aromatic golden chicken pieces
in an incredible creamy curry sauce.

Ingredients:

  • Boneless chicken breasts
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 teaspoons turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Instructions:

  1. Combine chicken with ingredients to make marinade, let it marinate for 30 minutes
  2. Heat oil in a skillet then toss the marinated chicken into the skillet, allow it to cook 3 minutes on each side.
  3. Heat butter and ghee in the same pan, then add garlic and saute for 1 minute.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes and chilli powder into the skillet and let it simmer for 15 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat and then blend mixutre and finally garnish with chopped cilantro and serve.

Grilled Cheese

Description:

Grilled cheese is a classic American sandwich that has been around since 1920.
It is a hot sandwich made with buttered and toasted bread and originally
filled with American cheese, but is now commonly made with one or more different cheeses.

Ingredients:

  • 2 pieces of bread
  • 1/2 Tsbp Butter
  • Medium Cheese

Instructions:

  1. Butter the toast
  2. Heat the skillet
  3. Add cheese to the toast then flip the sandwich
  4. Cook until the toast gets a brown coloring
  5. Cut the sandwich in half and serve
